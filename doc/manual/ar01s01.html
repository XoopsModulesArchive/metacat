1. Introduction

MetaCat brings together category management from a number of different modules. Categories can be created, edited, added or removed from individual modules, and moved to a differen place within the hierarchy.

Development of Metacat was funded by Stuurgroep Experimenten Volkshuisvesting, Netherlands for wijkwebsites.nl
